Manage My SurgeryTM (MMS) is a secure and HIPAA complaint platform created doctors to support patients and their caregivers throughout their entire surgery or medical procedure experience, form planning to recovery and rehabilitation.
Our Vision is to empower patients to help better manage their surgeries by offering them an easy-to-use, evidenced-based management tool that serves as a virtual navigator.
MMS also provides doctors and members of the care team an easy-to-use platform to monitor and enhance patient engagement.
